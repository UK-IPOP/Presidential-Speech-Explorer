UNITED STATES CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION,
The President.
SIR: The Commission recommends that Special Departmental Rule No. I be amended by adding to the exceptions from examination therein declared the following:
"11. In the Department of Justice: Assistant attorneys.
"12. In the Department of Agriculture, Bureau of Experiment Stations: Private secretary to the Director."
Very respectfully,
CHAS. LYMAN,
Approved, February 11, 1889.
GROVER CLEVELAND
