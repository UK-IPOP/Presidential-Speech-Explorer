By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5791 of February 2, 1932, withdrawing public lands in T. 10 S., R. 94 W. of the sixth principal meridian, Colorado, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.
This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of the said township.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
