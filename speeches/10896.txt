SECRETARY OF STATE HENRY L. STIMSON
I WISH to convey to you my sincere congratulations. My appreciation is the deeper from knowing the complexity of the difficulties which confronted you over the past ten days and the able manner they have been surmounted. I hope you may now secure a real vacation.
HERBERT HOOVER
