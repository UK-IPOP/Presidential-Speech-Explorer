By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the Emergency Relief Appropriation Act of 1935 (49 Stat. 115), and Title V of the act of June 15, 1935, 49 Stat. 378, 383, the Secretary of Agriculture is hereby authorized, with funds allocated to him under the said Title V of the act of June 15, 1935, to acquire real property or any interest therein by purchase, condemnation, or otherwise, as he may deem necessary or advisable for game bird and animal refuges and for migratory bird sanctuaries and refuges.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
