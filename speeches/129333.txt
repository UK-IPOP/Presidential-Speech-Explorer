Today, Vice President Mike Pence participated in an interfaith dialogue with faith leaders of Islam, Buddhism, Christianity, Hinduism, and Confucianism at the conclusion of his tour of the Istiqlal Mosque in Jakarta, Indonesia. Vice President Pence expressed appreciation to Indonesia for its shared commitment to religious freedom and reaffirmed that the United States will continue to defend this fundamental freedom, at home and abroad.

The list of dialogue participants was as follows:

 – Head of Istiqlal, Mosque Management Body

 – Grand Imam of Istiqlal Mosque

 – Vice Chairman of the Executive Board, the Istiqlal Mosque

 – General Chairperson, Communion of Churches in Indonesia (CCI/PGI)

 – Chairman for International Affairs, Parisada Hindu Dharma Indonesia (PHDI)

 – General Chairman of Supreme Council of Confucian, Religion in Indonesia (MATAKIN)

 – Catholic Archbishop of Jakarta

 – Former Secretary General, Indonesian Buddhists Association (Walubi)

 – Secretary General, Muhammadiyah

 – Chair of Muhammadiyah

 – Secretary General, Nahdlatul Ulama (NU)

 – Chair of Nahdlatul Ulama (NU)

 – Chairman of Advisory Board of the MUI

 – Director, The Wahid Institute

 – Member of the Betawi Cultural Development Forum

 – Head of Research and Development, Indonesian Society for Organization of Islamic Cooperation (ISOIC)

 – Secretary General of the Indonesian Society for Organization of Islamic Cooperation (ISOIC)
