

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and laws of the United States of America, including section 301 of title 3 of the United States Code, I hereby delegate to you the functions vested in me by section 562A(b)(3) of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1991 (Public Law 101-513), relating to the submission of a report to the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the President pro tempore of the Senate regarding possible NCR cooperation with the Khmer Rouge. The authority delegated by this memorandum may be further redelegated within the Department of State.
