[Released October 25, 1931. Dated October 23, 1931]
PLEASE present my hearty congratulations to the members of the Church of the Good Shepherd, Brooklyn, on its twenty-fifth anniversary, and all good wishes for the continuance of its service in behalf of spiritual advancement in the community.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Rev. William F. Sunday, 7420 Ridge Blvd., Brooklyn, N.Y.]
