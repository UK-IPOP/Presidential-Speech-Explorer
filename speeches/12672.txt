By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by paragraph Eighth, subdivision SECOND, section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is ordered that paragraph 6, Subdivision VII, Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules be, and it is hereby, amended to read as follows:
"6. Temporary clerks, carriers, and laborers required for part-time or intermittent work in the Postal Service in connection with the holiday or seasonal business from November 15, 1936, to January 15, 1937."
