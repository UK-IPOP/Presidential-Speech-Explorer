TALLAHASSEE, FL -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that North Carolina Veterans for McCain will hold a rally in Raleigh on Tuesday, July 22nd at 12:00 p.m. EDT. This rally occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across the country.
