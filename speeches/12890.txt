By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6076 of March 15, 1933, withdrawing, together with other lands, public lands in T. 1 S., R. 16 W., and T. 9 S., R. 9 W. of the New Mexico principal meridian, New Mexico, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said townships.
This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
