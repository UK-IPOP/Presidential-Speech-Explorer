ARLINGTON, VA -- Today, McCain-Palin 2008 released its latest television ad, entitled "Freedom." The ad features John McCain speaking directly to the American people about his belief in this country and his plan to build a stronger America by growing our economy and cutting waste. In this election, the American people are offered a clear choice -- hope for a stronger America or the knowledge that we will build a stronger America because only John McCain has the experience and leadership to do so. The ad will be televised in key states.
