[Released March 1, 1933. Dated February 21, 1933]
THE TENTH ANNIVERSARY of the American Braille Press is heartening evidence of the progress made in providing reading matter for the blind, opening the windows of the world to sightless eyes, and bringing to otherwise closed minds the joy and inspirations of world thought and experience.
HERBERT HOOVER
[David Resnick, American Braille Press, Inc., 598 Madison Avenue, New York, N.Y-]
