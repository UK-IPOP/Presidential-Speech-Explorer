President George W. Bush announced today the recess appointment of the following individuals:
Anthony Joseph Principi, of California, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission. The President has designated him Chairman.
James H. Bilbray, of Nevada, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
Philip Coyle, of California, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
Admiral Harold W. Gehman, Jr., USN (Ret.), of Virginia, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
Brigadier General Sue Ellen Turner, USAF (Ret.), of Texas, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
James V. Hansen, of Utah, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
General James T. Hill, USA (Ret.), of Florida, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
Samuel Knox Skinner, of Illinois, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
General Lloyd Warren Newton, USAF (Ret.), of Connecticut, to be a Member of the Defense Base Closure and Realignment Commission.
Michael W. Wynne, of Florida, to be Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition, Technology and Logistics.
