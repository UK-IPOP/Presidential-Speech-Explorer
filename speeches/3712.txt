In compliance with a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 18th of February last, requesting me to communicate to the House "the report of Captain E. B. Boutwell, and all the documents accompanying it, relative to the operations of the United States sloop of war 
 , under his command, at the Fejee Islands in the year 1855," I transmit herewith a report of the Secretary of the Navy.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
