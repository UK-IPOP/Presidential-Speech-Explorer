UNDER THE AUSPICES of the Advanced Research Projects Agency of the Department of Defense, a powerful, high-performance Atlas ICBM vehicle was launched into orbit from the Atlantic Missile Range at Cape Canaveral, Florida, at 6:02 p.m. today. The entire vehicle is in orbit.

This launching constitutes a distinct step forward in space operations. The success opens new opportunities to the United States and all mankind for activities in outer space.
