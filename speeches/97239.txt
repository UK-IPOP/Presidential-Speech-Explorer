President George W. Bush today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Belfast to attend the U.S. - Northern Ireland Investment Conference in Northern Ireland.
The Honorable Paula J. Dobriansky, Special Envoy to Northern Ireland, who also serves as the Under Secretary of State for Democracy and Global Affairs, will lead the delegation.
Members of the Presidential Delegation:
The Honorable Robert H. Tuttle, United States Ambassador to the United Kingdom
The Honorable Thomas C. Foley, United States Ambassador to Ireland
The Honorable Michael R. Bloomberg, Mayor of the City of New York
David Scott, President & CEO, 3PAR Inc.
Catherine Brune, Senior Vice President and CIO, Allstate Insurance Company
