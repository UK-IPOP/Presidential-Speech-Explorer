Arriving in Wilmington, Delaware, ahead of schedule, Bernie Sanders pulled his campaign caravan to a stop at a family-owned Italian restaurant, Mrs. Robino's. Over a plate of meatballs slathered with tomato sauce, the senator from Vermont answered questions from other restaurant patrons about his friendship with Delaware's favorite son, Vice President Joe Biden.
