This is the schedule of campaign events for Governor Bill Richardson for March 29 through April 6.
Thursday evening the Governor will be in El Paso to receive a "Sembrador del Bien" Award for his work on behalf of agriculture, human rights, and migrant farm workers. Governor Richardson will return to New Mexico late Thursday night.
On Friday evening the governor will travel to the Dallas area in Texas for a fundraiser. He will overnight in Texas and will return to New Mexico on Saturday afternoon.
Next week Governor Richardson will be in New Hampshire for a series of campaign activities, including several town meetings where the Governor will give brief remarks followed by a question and answer session with residents. Governor Richardson will also be speaking to a group of College Democrats at Dartmouth College. Please note the New Hampshire schedule may change and events may be added. The Governor will return to New Mexico Thursday night, April 5.
