President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint four individuals and nominate four individual to serve in his administration:
The President intends to nominate the following individuals to be Members of the National Security Education Board for a four-year term:
James William Carr of Arkansas
George M. Dennison of Montana
Andrew J. McKenna, Jr. of Illinois
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the National Infrastructure Advisory Council:
Rebecca F. Denlinger of Georgia
Gregory A. Peters of Georgia
Bruce Rohde of Nebraska
The President intends to appoint Randall L. Stephenson, of Texas, to be a Member of the President's National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee.
The President intends to nominate Harry Robinson, Jr., of Texas, to be a Member of the Museum and Library Services Board (Library), for a five-year term.
