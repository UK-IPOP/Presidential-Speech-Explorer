 Senator Joe Biden will kick off a two-day, five-city campaign trip across the Granite State on Friday, May 11th, 2007, in an address to the Federation of State and Provincial Fire Fighters Associations Bi-annual Meeting in Portsmouth. Throughout the weekend, Biden will bring his campaign to the living rooms of Merrimack County at several house parties hosted by local lawmakers and activists. He will also march in the Franklin Community Day Parade and meet with residents of the Golden Crest Assisted Living Facility.
