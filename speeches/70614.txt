The Administration supports the objective of H.R. 3899 -- to improve homeownership opportunities for all Americans. The bill would complement many of the Administration's own efforts to expand homeownership. The Administration is especially pleased that H.R. 3899 would reauthorize the HOME program and the Neighborhood Reinvestment Corporation and create Homeownership Zones to promote development in distressed neighborhoods.
