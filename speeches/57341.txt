



Pursuant to Public Law 101-513, the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1991, as made applicable to FY 1992 by the Joint Resolution on Further Continuing Appropriations, Fiscal Year 1992 (Public Law 102-145) (the Appropriations Laws), I hereby certify that it is in the national interest to obligate funds appropriated for payment to the International Development Association during FY 1992 but previously withheld from obligation under the Appropriations Laws.
