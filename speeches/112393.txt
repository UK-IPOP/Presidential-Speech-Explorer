Today, Romney for President released a new television advertisement titled "First 100 Days: Ohio." During President Romney's first 100 days, he will stand up to China and repeal regulations that are costing us jobs. For Ohio, these new policies will make the state a better place to do business.

 "President Romney's First 100 Days. For Ohio, already a better place to do business as we see more factories and jobs coming back."
 "I'm Mitt Romney and I approve this message."
