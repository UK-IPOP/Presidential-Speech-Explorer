[ Released November 16, 1959. Dated November 14, 1959 ]
Dear Mr. President:
Thank you for your very kind message. I am happy to be able to accept the invitation of your Government to come to India, and thus to fulfill a long-standing wish on my part. I am looking forward with great pleasure to meeting you and to the opportunity for paying my respects to the people and Government of India.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
