President Donald J. Trump today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to Strasbourg, France and Speyer, Germany to attend the Funeral of Dr. Helmut J. M. Kohl, Former German Chancellor and Citizen of Honor of Europe on July 1, 2017.

The Honorable Daniel Ray Coats, Director of National Intelligence and former Ambassador to Germany, will lead the delegation.
