President George W. Bush announced today that he has named two individuals to serve in his Administration:
The President has named Jeremy Katz to be Special Assistant to the President for Policy. Mr. Katz recently served as Special Assistant to the Chief of Staff for Policy at the White House. He received his bachelor's degree from the University of Pennsylvania.
The President has named Myriah Jordan to be Special Assistant to the President for Policy. Ms. Jordan recently served as Special Assistant to the Chief of Staff for Policy at the White House. She received her bachelor's from John Brown University and her JD from the University of Texas School of Law.
