Announcement was made of the appointment of the Commission created by the Act approved June 12, 1934, entitled "An Act to Revise Airmail Laws and to Establish a Commission to Make a Report to the Congress Recommending an Aviation Policy." The following will constitute this Commission:
Clark Howell, of Atlanta, Georgia
Jerome Clarke Hunsaker, of New York
Edward P. Warner, of Washington, D.C.
Franklin K. Lane, Jr., of California
Albert J. Berres, of California
