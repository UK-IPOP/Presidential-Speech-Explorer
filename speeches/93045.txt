SANTA FE, NM-- New Mexico Governor and Democratic Presidential candidate Bill Richardson will be traveling to Iowa this week to campaign. On Monday, December 3rd, Governor Richardson will visit his 99th and final county in Iowa and host a Presidential Job Interview at the Copper Lantern Restaurant in Keokuk County. On Tuesday, December 4th, he will attend the funeral of Corporal Clem Boody, whose remains he brought back from North Korea earlier this year.
Governor Richardson also will be travelling to Washington, D.C. to campaign and fundraise.
