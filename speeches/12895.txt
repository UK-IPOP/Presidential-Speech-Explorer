By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, 37 Stat. 497, the Executive Order of September 23, 1912, entitled "Mineral Land Withdrawal No. 1, Arizona No. 1," withdrawing public lands in Arizona for classification and in aid of legislation, is hereby revoked in so far as it affects the lands described as Lots 1 to 14, inclusive, and NW¼ SE¼ Sec. 37, T. 23 S., R. 24 E., Gila and Salt River Meridian.
This order is made for the purpose of permitting title to all of the lands not otherwise reserved to vest, subject to valid existing claims, in the State of Arizona under the provisions of the act of July 22, 1854, 10 Stat. 308, the act of February 24, 1863, 12 Stat. 604, and the act of June 20, 1910, 36 Stat. 572.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
