Released April 21, 1932. Dated April 19, 1932]
My dear Mr. Markham:
I send you my most hearty congratulations upon your 80th birthday, and my hope that you may long continue to inspire the American people with your gift of song. Mrs. Hoover joins with me in sentiments of cordial regards both to yourself and Mrs. Markham.

Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Edwin Markham, Westerleigh Park, West New Brighton, N.Y.]
