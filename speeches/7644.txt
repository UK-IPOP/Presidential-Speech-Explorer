[Released September 5, 1930. Dated September 4, 1930]
I AM SHOCKED and grieved to learn of the terrible storm disaster which has wrought such devastation in Santo Domingo and I hasten to send you my own deep sympathy and that of my fellow citizens with your suffering people.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency General Rafael L. Trujillo, President of the Dominican Republic, Santo Domingo]
