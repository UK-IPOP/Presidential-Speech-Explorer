[Released May 3, 1931. Dated May 1, 1931]
I WILL be obliged if you will express my cordial greetings to those present at the Sixteenth Anniversary Dinner of the American Jewish Congress and my appreciation of the many valuable contributions made to our National life by our citizens of the Jewish race.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Bernard G. Richards, Executive Director, American Jewish Congress, 33 West 42nd Street, New York City]
