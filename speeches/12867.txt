By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6286 of September 14, 1933, withdrawing public lands in Tps. 9 and 10 S., R. 8 W. of the New Mexico principal meridian, New Mexico, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.
This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
