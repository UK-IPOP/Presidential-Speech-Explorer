A new muscle-crushing, sweat-inducing workout has infiltrated the inner sanctum of Congress, the members-only gym of the House of Representatives. And it is called, simply, the "Markwayne Mullin."

Named after Mr. Mullin, the 36-year-old Republican representative from Oklahoma and former professional 
 cage fighter who leads the predawn, bespoke workout, it is a "mix of CrossFit and circuit training," he says.
