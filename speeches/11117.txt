[Released April 23, 1931. Dated April 21, 1931]
I THANK Your Majesty for your telegram and assure you that it has been a sincere pleasure to have as our guests your distinguished brother and his Princess Consort. The cordial friendship evinced by our imperial guests have completely won our hearts and their visit will remain a most pleasant memory.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty Hirohito, Emperor of Japan, Tokyo]
