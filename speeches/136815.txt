On Friday, December 20, 2019, the President signed into law:

H.R. 1158, the "Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2020," which divisions A through D of the enrolled bill provides full-year funding through September 30, 2020, for projects and activities of certain agencies of the Federal Government; and

H.R. 1865, the "Further Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2020," which Divisions A through H of the enrolled bill provides full-year funding through September 30, 2020, for projects and activities of the remaining agencies of the Federal Government.
