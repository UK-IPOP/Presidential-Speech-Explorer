On Friday, August 23, 2019, the President signed into law:

H.R. 1250, which designates the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 11158 Highway 146 North in Hardin, Texas, as the Lucas Lowe Memorial Post Office; and

H.R. 3245, the "Restore the Harmony Way Bridge Act," which transfers ownership of the Harmony Way Bridge from the White County Bridge Commission to the States of Illinois and Indiana.
