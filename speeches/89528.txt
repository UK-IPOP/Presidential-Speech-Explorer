The President today announced his intention to appoint seven individuals to serve in his Administration.
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to be Members of the President's Commission on White House Fellowships:
Leanna Brown, of New Jersey
Les T. Csorba, of Texas
Joseph V. Del Raso, of Pennsylvania
Richard S. Mroz, of New Jersey
Joseph E. Samora, of New Mexico
Justin J. Sayfie, of Florida
Gordon D. Sondland, of Washington
