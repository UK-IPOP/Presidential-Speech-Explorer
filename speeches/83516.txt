President George W. Bush today announced his intention to nominate one individual to serve in his administration:
The President intends to nominate Raymond F. DuBois, of the District of Columbia, to be Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Logistics and Material Readiness. Mr. DuBois currently serves as Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for Installations and Environment. He previously served as Special Assistant to the Secretary and Deputy Secretary of Defense. Earlier in his career, Mr. DuBois was President of Potomac Strategies International, a consulting firm that provides strategic management solutions to companies in the aerospace, electronics, telecommunications, and telemedicine industries. He received his bachelor's degree from Princeton University.
