By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 4, 1897, 30 Stat. 34, 36 (U.S.C., title 16, sec. 473), it is ordered that the description of the land contained in Proclamation No. 2190 of July 17, 1936, establishing the Talladega National Forest, Alabama, be, and it is hereby, modified so that the land described therein as being in T. 20 S., R. 4 E., section 35, Huntsville Meridian, shall be described as being in T. 20 S., R. 4 E., section 25, Huntsville Meridian.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
