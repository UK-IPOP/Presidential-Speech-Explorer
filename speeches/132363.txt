President Donald J. Trump today announced the following White House appointments:

. Mr. Davids previously served as Deputy Commander, Naval Special Warfare Command.

. Mr. Fuentes previously served as Military Assistant to the Secretary of Homeland Security.

. Ms. Nielsen formerly served as Chief of Staff of the Department of Homeland Security.

. Mr. Peede previously served as Deputy Assistant to the Vice President and Director of Vice Presidential Advance.

. Mr. Walsh previously served as Deputy Director, White House Military Office.
