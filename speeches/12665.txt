By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of the United States and by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, it is ordered that Executive Order No. 2124 of January 20, 1915, establishing for the protection of native birds the Ediz Hook Reservation, Washington, containing approximately 83 acres, on an arm of land in the Straits of Juan de Fuca extending from and adjoining Port Angeles Townsite in T. 31 N., R. 6 W., Willamette Meridian, be, and it is hereby, revoked.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
