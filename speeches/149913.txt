The Vice President landed at 11:15 a.m. CST at Ellington Field Joint Reserve Base in Houston. VP was greeted on the tarmac by:
- Col. Andrew Camacho, Wing Commander, 147th Attack Wing, Ellington Field Joint Reserve Base
- MSgt. Trang Maxie, Wing Command Chief, 147th Attack Wing, Ellington Field Joint Reserve Base
- Harris County Judge Lina Hidalgo, (D-TX)
VP arrived at noon at the Hilton Americas-Houston to speak at the National Baptist Convention. Houston Mayor Sylvester Turner and Harris County Commissioner Rodney Ellis are among those in attendance.
VP began remarks at 12:32 pm at the National Baptist Convention and spoke for 23 minutes, urging people of faith to help lead the country through "unsettled times" and defend its democracy. More than 2,000 people were in attendance, according to event organizers. More speech highlights to follow.
