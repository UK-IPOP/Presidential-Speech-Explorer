By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Orders No. 6122 of May 2, 1933, and No. 6266 of September 6, 1933, withdrawing public lands in T.1 S., R. 83 W., and T. 1 S., R. 84 W. of the sixth principal meridian, Colorado, respectively, pending a resurvey, are hereby revoked.
This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships.
FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT
