President Bush Announced His Designation of Eleven Individuals to Represent the United States at the Inauguration of the President of the Republic of Nicaragua
President George W. Bush today announced his designation of eleven individuals to represent the United States at the Inauguration of his Excellency Enrique Bolanos Geyer, President of the Republic of Nicaragua on January 10, 2002.
The following individuals will be members of the delegation representing the United States at the Inauguration of his Excellency Enrique Bolanos Geyer, President of the Republic of Nicaragua.
The Honorable Mel Martinez, Secretary of Housing and Urban Development will serve as the leader of the delegation.
