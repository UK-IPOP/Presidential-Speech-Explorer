President George W. Bush today announced his intention to appoint fourteen individuals to serve in his administration.
The President intends to appoint the following individuals to the President's Advisory Commission on Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders:
John B. Tsu of California
Lupo Carlota of Tennessee
David B. Cohen of California
Mary Ling of California
Barbara Marumoto of Hawaii
Gary K. Ong of Arizona
Sunny Park of Georgia
Amata Coleman Radewagen of America Somoa
Jhoon Rhee of Virginia
Michelle Park Steel of California
Joe Ting of Texas
Chiling Tong of California
Baoky N. Vu of Georgia
Zachariah Zachariah of Florida
