This is the schedule of campaign events for Governor Bill Richardson for March 21 through 24.  
Today and tomorrow Governor Richardson will be in California and Arizona for a series of private campaign events before returning to Santa Fe Thursday evening.  On Friday night and Saturday, the Governor will be in Las Vegas, Nevada to attend a rally for the Culinary Workers of America, participate in a Presidential candidate forum sponsored by the Service Employees International Union (SEIU), and deliver a speech to the National Convention of the Stonewall Democrats. Saturday night the Governor will deliver the keynote address at the Human Rights Campaign Gala Dinner in Los Angeles, California, and will return to New Mexico late that night.
